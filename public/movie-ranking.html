The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Bookstore by the Cloudy Side

A touching family story that records the daily trivialities of life.

My Neighbor Totoro

Two sisters encounter a giant Totoro in the countryside, embarking on a fantastical and healing story filled with innocence and the breath of nature.

Spirited Away

Young girl Chihiro strays into the spirit world, works in a bathhouse, and through hardships, finally finds herself and rescues her parents.
